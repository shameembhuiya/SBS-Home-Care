FAQ

Common questions about home care

How quickly can care begin?
In most cases, care can begin within 24–48 hours of your initial call, after a free consultation and care plan review.
Is SBS Home Care licensed and insured?
Yes — SBS Home Care is a licensed home care agency, and every caregiver is insured, bonded, and background-checked before placement.
Does Medicaid or insurance cover home care?
Many families combine Medicaid, long-term care insurance, and private pay. We'll walk through your specific options during your free consultation.
Do you offer flexible or hourly schedules?
Yes. We offer hourly, extended-shift, respite, and live-in care to match your family's exact needs.
Can families help choose the caregiver?
Absolutely. Family input shapes the care plan and caregiver match — we want the fit to feel right for everyone.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the Bronx, Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Westchester, and Yonkers in New York, plus Fairfield and New Haven Counties in Connecticut.
